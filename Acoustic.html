Acoustic Treatment

Acoustic treatment is one of the most important aspects of planning and building a room where sound quality and noise control are paramount.Prime examples are spaces such as recording studios, games rooms, and Home theatres.

Without it, you risk excess noise ingress and sound reverberation. This is why you need to fully understand the concept of acoustic treatment to produce a high-end result.
